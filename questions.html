Guide Questions Based on
“The Transformative Role of Networks in Modern Society”
In what ways do social media platforms rely on computer networks to enable real-time communication, instant content sharing, live interactions, and global connectivity among users from different countries and cultures, and how do network speed, reliability, and global infrastructure contribute to these functions?
How do computer networks support online learning platforms, virtual classrooms, and digital educational tools, and why are these network-based tools important for improving accessibility, collaboration, and flexibility in modern education?
How have networks evolved from simple tools for data transfer into complex systems that influence communication, business operations, education, healthcare, and social relationships?
Do you think the development of high-speed networks has changed how people communicate and interact on social media across different cultures and countries? Why or why not?
